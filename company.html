The Architecture

Three Pillars. One Platform.

Every tool your school needs — Performance, Welfare, and Curriculum — engineered to work as one.

Performance Centre

Daily register with FIFA 6-pillar scoring, resilience tracking, HT Command dashboard, room heatmap, morning reports, and player cards. Your school’s performance data — clear, live, and actionable.

Welfare Suite

DSL sovereignty toggle, NODE correlation engine, safeguarding chronology, 3-path triage, SLT response hub, and a complete audit trail. Built to the standard your students deserve.

Kit Room

Resource Bible, taxonomy management, partner links, curriculum unit cascade, opportunity reports, and the Ticker Board. Everything your team needs to close the gap.

Why Arena

Purpose-Built for Your Context

Not adapted. Not repurposed. Designed from day one for the realities of Alternative Provision.

Sovereign Data Architecture

Your welfare data stays under DSL control. No third-party access. No cloud-first compromise. The Arena Hub cannot read your clinical records.

Provider-Agnostic AI

Human teachers and AI agents are treated identically within your ecosystem. Evidence assessment, letter drafting, NODE correlation — all transparent and auditable.

Hybrid Infrastructure

Google Workspace backbone with Cloud Run orchestration. No new logins for your team. Deploys into your existing environment in under an hour.

2FA Everywhere It Counts
