Clinical Infrastructure — Three Pillars

Distinct function.
Unified chronology.

Each Pillar is a sovereign, permission-controlled clinical unit. Together they produce the only evidence infrastructure in the UK AP market purpose-built for SEND reform compliance.

01
Pillar 01 — Mandatory at All Tiers
Performance Centre
Academic Velocity Engine
Imagine a live scoreboard for every student's learning journey. Staff record what a student did and how well — the Performance Centre turns those daily moments into a clear, running picture of progress that anyone from a class teacher to a Local Authority can understand at a glance.

Core Sheet

MASTER_PERFORMANCE_CENTRE — hosts all three Vaults (Student, Staff, Parent), Academic Track, Playbook, and Squad Overview. Mandatory at all tier levels (T1_PC through T3_FULL).

Key Functions

  • PERF_CORE_SYNC() — continuous Manifest push
  • VelocityScore — attainment speed metric
  • SQUAD_OVERVIEW — fast cross-pillar roster view
  • THE_STUDENT_VAULT — 12-column clinical record
  • THE_STAFF_VAULT — 13-column RBAC registry

Clinical Value

Produces the Academic Velocity chronology that underpins EHCP Section B progress evidence, Annual Review packs, and LA placement justification. Real-time feed to LA Portfolio tier.

02
Pillar 02 — DSL-Silo Architecture
Welfare Suite
Forensic Immutability Protocol — VAR Sentry Active
A completely sealed, secure space where safeguarding concerns are recorded and locked in time. Only the designated safeguarding lead can access it. Every entry is permanent, timestamped, and tamper-proof — so that if anyone ever questions what happened and when, the answer is already there in black and white.

Core Sheet

MASTER_WELFARE_SUITE — safeguarding chronology, forensic audit trail. Access restricted to DSL and registered Organizer. Siloed subfolder with revoked inheritance ensures no staff cross-access.

VAR Sentry Specification

  • Installable onEdit() trigger — not simple trigger
  • getLastRow()+1 append model — tamper-evident
  • Logs: TIMESTAMP, USER, SHEET, CELL, PREV_VAL, NEW_VAL
  • Hidden VAR_AUDIT_LOG — inaccessible to school staff
  • Named after Video Assistant Referee — immutable review

Clinical Value

Produces a legally defensible safeguarding chronology. At tribunal, every welfare entry is timestamped and user-attributed. At Ofsted inspection, the VAR log is the single source of truth for safeguarding governance.

03
Pillar 03 — AI Assessor Engine
The Kit Room
Clinical Assessor AI — Vocational Evidence Mapping
The place where practical learning becomes provable achievement. A student builds a car engine, cooks a meal, or completes a project — the Kit Room turns that real-world activity into formal, nationally recognised evidence. Our built-in AI does the heavy lifting of matching what the student did to the qualification criteria. The teacher makes the final call, always.

Core Sheet

MASTER_KIT_ROOM — vocational project log (PBLLOG), criteria map (CRITERIAMAP), stakeholder CRM, AI planner. Powered by Gemini 1.5 Flash via Apps Script integration.

Explainability Return Schema

  • MET criteria codes (e.g. P1, M1, D1)
  • JUSTIFICATION — 2-sentence rationale
  • MISSING — evidence gap for next level
  • CONFIDENCE_SCORE — percentage (0–100%)
  • HUMAN_VERIFICATION_FLAG — Pending/Verified/Returned

Clinical Value

Closes the BTEC evidence gap in AP settings. Practical work is formally mapped against national standards within seconds, with full explainability documentation. Human assessor retains final authority over all grading decisions.

Master Manifest — Central Controller

All three Pillars are connected via the Master Manifest — a developer-owned, version-agnostic central registry. Updates propagate automatically to every deployed Arena instance. The Manifest holds the blueprint. The school owns the evidence.

Provision Integrity — Before vs. After Arena

The evidence gap
is a liability. Arena closes it.

This is not a feature comparison. It is a forensic audit of what happens to a student's record — and an LA's legal exposure — in the absence of evidence integrity infrastructure.

Before Arena — Current State

The Evidence Gap

An AP student's academic progress exists in a teacher's private spreadsheet. Their welfare events are recorded — if at all — in a shared folder with no access control and no audit trail. Their vocational attainment is undocumented in any format that a BTEC assessor, Ofsted inspector, or LA commissioner could interrogate.


When the EHCP annual review arrives, the evidence pack is assembled manually over two days, drawing on memory, informal notes, and whatever files can be located. When a placement is challenged at tribunal, the school cannot produce a timestamped chronology of provision. The LA cannot defend the decision.


The case is lost. The cost follows.

vs
After Arena — Evidence Integrity

The Immutable Chronology

The moment a student is added to the system, their evidence chronology begins. Every lesson observation is timestamped. Every welfare event is logged to an immutable audit trail, restricted to the DSL and forensically complete. Every vocational session is AI-mapped against national criteria within seconds, with explainability documentation — criteria citations, confidence scores, human-verification flags — attached to every entry.


When the annual review arrives, the evidence pack generates automatically. When Ofsted inspect, the read-only inspection view requires no preparation. When a placement is challenged at tribunal, Arena produces an unbroken, timestamped, tamper-evident chronology from day one of provision.


The case is not lost. The student is protected. The provision is defensible.

Data Architecture — How Evidence Travels

Three pillars at the centre.
Evidence flows outward.

The Arena's three pillars act as a single, pulsing engine. Evidence generated every day — in lessons, in welfare conversations, in workshops — is collected, organised, and pushed outward as encrypted, structured data to Local Authorities who need to see it. No manual reports. No emails. Just clean, protected data flowing where it needs to go.

Each pillar feeds evidence into the Arena's central controller. The Arena encrypts and packages that data, then delivers it outward — to Local Authorities, Ofsted, and legal proceedings — only what each recipient is authorised to see.
