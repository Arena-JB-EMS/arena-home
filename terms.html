2. The Service

The Arena Hub is a cloud-based Education Management System (EMS) built specifically for Alternative Provision (AP) settings in the United Kingdom. It comprises three integrated pillars:

  • Performance Centre — student registration, attendance, FIFA-style progress scoring, EHCP evidence tracking, and reporting.
  • Welfare Suite — DSL-grade safeguarding case management, welfare chronology, and AI-assisted letter drafting.
  • Kit Room — physical resource management, vocational qualification tracking, and partner course registration.

The platform runs on Google Apps Script, with school data stored within your organisation’s own Google Workspace environment. Access tiers determine which pillars are available to your school.

We reserve the right to update, improve, or modify the platform at any time. We will provide reasonable notice of any changes that materially affect your use of the service.
